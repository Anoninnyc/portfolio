About Me

I'm partial to programming that engages my algorithmic thinking, and have a penchant for finding the optimal means to solve front and back-end problems: whether it’s finding a way to implement an AngularJS/ReactJS-based UI in the fewest keystrokes, or reducing the time cost of a DB query- so long as the work ahead of me provides a unique set of challenges, I’m doing what I love. I’ve recently been working on a myriad of applications, utilizing some of today’s most popular frameworks and libraries. The apps I’ve built and contributed to incorporate Data Visualization, Real-Time Communication, along with a host of other popular development trends.
